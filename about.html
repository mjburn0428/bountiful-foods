Who We Are

We are local

We are based in Carlsbad California. Carlsbad is between the greater Los Angeles Area and San Diego Area.

We provide delicious flavors

All of our produce is grown locally. We partner with local farmers to provide the best locally grown produce to better serve you as our customers.

We cater to all individuals

Our purpose and mission is to cater to all persons old and young alike. We want to provide the healthly alternative to enjoying fruits and our drink options are the best we have to offer.

We don't have a formal menu

We do not have a menu at all. We want our customers to enjoy all that we have to offer. We provide the ingridents to your drink. You as the customer add the ingridents into your drink. We make it and provide you with your custom drink.
